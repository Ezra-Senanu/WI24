Ezra Senanu is an accomplished fashion, advertising, graduation, and lifestyle photographer & cinematographer based in Rexburg, Idaho, USA. He founded Ecks Photos in 2020, and has since established a reputation as one of the most inspiring leading photographers in his area.

Jeremy is a highly educated professional, having graduated in the top 1% of all University post-graduates in any field for his Master of Digital Media from the University of Adelaide, after receiving a Bachelor of Design and a Bachelor of Architecture. He spent a decade working as an architect for some of Australia’s top firms, as well as a digital media lecturer, all while developing his skills as a professional fashion and beauty photographer. Over the years, Jeremy has worked with a range of highly respected clients, including Australia’s Next Top Model, Tag Heuer, Thrills, Myer, MJ Bale, the Socceroos, Independent Skate Co, Novo Shoes, Santa Cruz, Wrangler, and One Teaspoon to name a few. He has also had the opportunity to shoot with a number of high-profile celebrities, such as the late Olivia Newton-John, Celeste Barber, Chris Hemsworth, Flume, Lorna Jane, Rose Byrne, and Stephanie Gilmore. Jeremy is also a highly sought-after photographer for events, having shot regular events for Stab Mag, Afends, and the AFL. His work has been featured in reputable publications, such as Body & Soul, Monster Children, Cosmopolitan, Sticks & Stones Agency, C-heads, Acclaim, Arsenic, and Off The Rails. Currently, Jeremy is the lead Asia Pacific fashion photographer for Billabong, Quiksilver, RVCA, Roxy, Surf Dive and Ski, Element, and DC. He is also the Byron Bay and Gold Coast photographer for Australia's premier youth culture website, Life Without Andy. With his extensive experience, artistic vision, and keen eye for detail, Jeremy is committed to providing his clients with stunning imagery that captures the essence of their brand or event.
